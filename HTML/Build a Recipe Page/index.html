Veg Momos

Welcome to the ultimate guide for making delicious veg momos! These soft, steamed dumplings filled with fresh vegetables are healthy, tasty, and perfect for snacks, parties, or light meals. Follow this easy recipe to make restaurant-style veg momos at home. Ingredients for cooking: chopped cabbage, carrots, onions, garlic, ginger, and spices arranged on a kitchen counter.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup finely chopped cabbage
  • 1/2 cup grated carrot
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1 teaspoon garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon oil

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour with water and knead into a soft dough. Let it rest for 20 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and sauté garlic, ginger, and onions until fragrant.
  3. Add cabbage, carrot, soy sauce, pepper, and salt. Cook for 2–3 minutes and let the mixture cool.
  4. Roll the dough into small circles and place a spoonful of vegetable filling in the center. Fold and shape the edges to form momos.
  5. Steam the momos for 10–12 minutes until fully cooked.
  6. Serve hot with spicy chili sauce or momos chutney and enjoy!
